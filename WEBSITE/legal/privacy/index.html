Privacy Policy.

This draft describes the intended minimal data model for VFxM purchases, app licensing, activations, and downloads.

Payment data

Payment data is handled by the configured store/payment provider. Virtue Creative Systems does not store credit card numbers or raw payment credentials.

Licensing data

The VFxM licensing backend stores minimal license and activation records needed for the app to activate, validate, deactivate, and support licenses. It avoids storing raw license keys and raw machine identifiers.

Stored records

The backend may store hashed identifiers, activation status, app version, platform, validation timestamps, provider order/license IDs, and download request metadata for licensing, security, and support.
