Refund Policy.

This draft must be reviewed against the final merchant-of-record policies before launch.

Sales final by default

Digital software sales are final by default. Refunds may be considered for duplicate purchases, delivery failure, or confirmed technical file/access issues.

Provider policy controls

Refund handling must not conflict with Lemon Squeezy or any configured merchant-of-record policies. Approved refunds may disable the related license.
