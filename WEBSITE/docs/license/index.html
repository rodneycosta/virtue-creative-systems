Licensing overview

Licensing exists. Activation lives in the app.

Your purchase includes a VFxM license/passkey. Activation happens inside Virtue FX Manager, directly in REAPER. The website handles purchase, download, documentation, and support.

Purchase

License/passkey delivery

The store provider delivers the license/passkey after checkout according to provider configuration.

Backend

Secure validation

The VFxM app talks to the backend license API. The public website does not validate licenses in the browser.

App

Grace and deactivation

Offline grace display, device status, diagnostics, and deactivation belong inside VFxM.

Do not paste your license/passkey into any public website form. Official activation happens only inside Virtue FX Manager after installation.
