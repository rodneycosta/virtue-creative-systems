Everything you need

Built for creative speed.

Every feature is designed to remove menu hunting and clutter, keeping your focus on the mix.

Visual Browser

Browse by Thumbnail, Not Lists

Reclaim your visual memory. Identify instruments and processors by their actual graphical interfaces.

Trade plain alphabetical text menu hunting for a rich, visual plugin dashboard. Open plugins in REAPER, capture their custom interfaces in one click with the built-in crop camera tool, and scroll through thumbnails that your brain recognizes instantly.

Instant Load

Accelerated Track Insertion

Stay in the zone. Load effects or instruments without breaking your creative flow.

Double-click to instantly load any plugin onto your active track, or drag a card directly into REAPER's arranger to auto-create track pathways. Select multiple plugins to insert entire signal chains sequentially in a single action.

Millisecond search

Advanced Filter Engine

Slice through large libraries. Filter by developer, format, or category in one tap.

Manage thousands of effects without breaking a sweat. Quickly focus your library using multi-column chips to filter by brand developer, plugin format (VST, VST3, AU, CLAP, JSFX), or functional categories like EQs, Compressors, and Reverbs.

Personalized Boards

Custom Favorites & Lists

Design custom layout boards for vocals, drums, mixing, or sound design.

Group your go-to mixing chains and instruments into custom tabs. Double-click tabs to rename them, drag horizontally to organize them, write persistent routing notes, and pin REAPER FX Chains for instant recall.

Workspace Control

Clutter Management Firewall

Non-destructively manage listed vs unlisted plugin visibility.

Hide trial formats, secondary wrappers, or utility plugins you don't need to see while mixing. Toggle status circles to list or unlist items instantly, keeping your main search workspace pristine and focused.

Automation Requires user interaction

Auto Thumbnail Scanner

Automatically build your visual library in minutes.

Skip the manual screenshots. The built-in scanner automatically runs in the background to load your plugins onto a temporary track, capture their custom graphical interfaces, and build a beautiful visual grid for you. It automatically ignores unlisted/hidden plugins, skips disconnected external drive paths, and blocklists unstable plugins to prevent DAW crashes.
